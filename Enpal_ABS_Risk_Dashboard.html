5-Step Loan Price Calculation — Complete Methodology
1
Monthly Annuity Payment — Standard Annuity Formula
Payment = P × [r(1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n − 1]   where r = APR/12 = 5.99%/12 = 0.4992%/month
Solar (n=300 months): 30,000 × formula = EUR 193/month ✓
Heat pump (n=180 months): 30,000 × formula = EUR 253/month ✓
2
Net Expected Loss (NEL) per SCHUFA Band
NEL = Expected Default Rate × (1 − Recovery Rate)   Recovery = 20% blended
SCHUFA A: 0.12% × 0.80 = 0.10%  |  SCHUFA H: 0.95% × 0.80 = 0.76%  |  Wtd avg: 0.14% p.a. ✓
3
Discount Rate — Investor Required Yield (constant across all bands)
Discount Rate = EURIBOR (~3.75%) + ABS spread (~1.43%) + Blended NEL (0.14%) = 5.32%
Per-band credit risk is already captured in NEL — discount rate is constant for all SCHUFA bands.
4
Present Value of All Future Cash Flows
PV = Payment × [1−(1+r_d)^−n / r_d] × (1−NEL)   r_d = 5.32%/12 = 0.4433%/month
Solar wtd avg (n=300, pmt=193): PV = EUR 30,898  |  Heat pump wtd avg (n=180, pmt=253): PV = EUR 30,641
5
Loan Price — The Missing Methodology (sourced & applied)
Loan Price (%) = Present Value / Loan Balance × 100
Solar wtd avg: 30,898 / 30,000 × 100 = 103.00% ✓  |  Heat pump wtd avg: 30,641 / 30,000 × 100 = 102.14% ✓
Above par because APR 5.99% > Discount rate 5.32% = +67 bps positive carry.
